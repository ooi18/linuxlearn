````

           TRANS  358613294320       0      0       0


               Maximum device load 183382 (Fri Sep 24 14:44:24 2021)

                        Relative Load in 1 sec(s) step

                         Received          27798
                         Transmitted       34958
                         Total load        62756


          ###

                                device eth0






````
